# Portfolio Design Plan - Aryan

## 1. Architecture & Design Overview
The portfolio will be built using **Next.js 14 (App Router)** for its performance and SEO benefits. We will use **Tailwind CSS** for styling, focusing on a **Glassmorphism** aesthetic.

**Design Highlights:**
- **Glassmorphism**: Translucent card backgrounds with `backdrop-blur`, subtle borders, and soft shadows.
- **Color Palette**: Deep dark backgrounds (mesh gradients) with accents of blue or purple to highlight the premium tech/engineering feel.
- **Typography**: Modern sans-serif (Inter or Outfit) for readability and a premium vibe.

## 2. Component Structure & Content
The site will follow a single-page layout for a fluid user experience.

### Sections:
- **Hero Section**: "Kazi Abu Aryan" - Focus on the Mechanical Engineering background and passion for technology.
- **About Me**: Your journey from Dawood Public School and College (Class of 2022) and Govt. Michael Madhusudan College (Class of 2024) in Jashore to studying Mechanical Engineering at SUST.
- **Projects**: 
    - **Autonomous Car Project**: Highlighting your role as a trainee and technical skills.
    - **Formula 1 Project**: A "Coming Soon" teaser to show your future goals.
- **Skills Grid**: Categories for Programming (C, Python) and Engineering Design (AutoCAD, SolidWorks).
- **Contact & Socials**: Integrated LinkedIn link and email contact.

## 3. Interaction & Technical Details
- **Animations**: Using **Framer Motion** for smooth entrances of glass cards and parallax effects on the background.
- **SEO**: Fully optimized meta tags for Aryan's professional brand.
- **Responsive**: Mobile-first design ensuring the glass effects look great on all devices.
- **Type Safety**: Full TypeScript implementation for robust code.
